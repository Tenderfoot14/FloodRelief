Property Damage

After a natural disaster, there is almost always property damage. There are government programs in place through agencies such as FEMA and the Small Business Administration that can provide assistance to residents and businesses with property damage as a result of natural disasters.

Resources

  • You can check if your home is applicable for FEMA assistance at DisasterRelief.gov or by calling 1-(800)-621-FEMA (3362).
  • Small Business owners who's business' were affected by the flooding should call 1-(800)-659-2955
