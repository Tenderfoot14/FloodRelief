Donations

If you wish to donate your time or money, please start by calling the state's donation team at 1-(888)-585-9643

Donate Money

The SC Emergency Management Division says the best way to aid disaster relief is to make a monetary contribution to one of these organizations

  • Red Cross of Central South Carolina - (800) 435-7669
  • Harvest Hope Food Bank - (803) 254-4432
  • Lutheran Disaster Response: (800) 638-3522
  • Presbyterian Disaster Assistance - (800) 872-3283
  • Southern Baptist Convention/Disaster Relief - (800) 462-8657, www.scbaptist.org
  • United Methodist Committee on Relief - (800) 554-8583
  • United Way of America - (800) 272-4630
  • United Way of South Carolina - (803) 929-1000
  • More at: www.scmed.org

Donate Water

The SC Emergency Management Commission says the state no longer needs donation of water.

Donate Supplies

  • Tripp's Fine Cleaners is accepting donations of clothing and bedding for flood victims, they ask it be sorted by size and gender.
  • The Newberry Sherrif's Office is accepting donations of food, cleaning supplies, and paper goods.
  • The Columbia Fireflies are accepting donations of canned food, baby items, and personal care items at the Team Store. (807 Gervais Street (Suite 100)) These supplies will go to United Way of the Midlands.
